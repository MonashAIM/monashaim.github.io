WANT TO JOIN AIM?

WANT TO
JOIN AIM?

Do you want to be part of the future? Monash AIM (Analysis of Images in Medicine) is hungry for passionate student volunteers! We are hell-bent on revolutionising medicine through AI-powered analysis of medical imaging. If this piques your interest, apply below!

Roles with Monash AIM will particularly favour students with:

- A background in medicine or coding
- Experience in committee roles
- A professional attitude
- Enthusiasm :)

As we have big dreams, we also have relatively big expectations for our volunteers. Please expect to be doing 5-10 hours of work each week to complete tasks.

RECRUITMENT IS OPEN


Unfortunately we are not actively recruiting as of now. But don't worry!

You can express interest with the form below!
