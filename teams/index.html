OUR TEAM

OUR TEAM

Leadership

The leadership team is structured to have two Leads for the organisation, one Lead for Development and one Lead for Education and Marketing.

Development

The development team will focus on research into medical conditions and developing imaging models for those conditions. The team is split into 2-3 groups which all focus on different projects, with one project per semester.

Education

The education team onboards new members, which involves hosting workshops and refining our resources.

Marketing

The marketing team focuses on updating the website and other external materials. The marketing team also leads event promotion and logistics, as well as recruitment.
